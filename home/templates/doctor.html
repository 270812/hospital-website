About Our Doctors

Dr Sanjay Gupta

Dr. Sanjay Gupta is a prominent figure in the medical field, known for his expertise in neurosurgery and his contributions to health journalism. Born on October 23, 1969, in Novi, Michigan, he earned his medical degree from the University of Michigan Medical School. Dr. Gupta is a practicing neurosurgeon and serves as an associate chief of the neurosurgery service at Grady Memorial Hospital in Atlanta, Georgia.

Dr Mehmet oz

Dr. Mehmet Oz, often referred to simply as Dr. Oz, is a Turkish-American cardiothoracic surgeon, author, and television personality. Born on June 11, 1960, in Cleveland, Ohio, Dr. Oz graduated from Harvard University and the University of Pennsylvania School of Medicine. He is board-certified in cardiothoracic surgery and has received numerous awards and honors for his contributions to the field of medicine..

Dr Anthony Fauci

Dr. Anthony Fauci is an American physician and immunologist who has become one of the most recognizable figures in public health, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic. Born on December 24, 1940, in Brooklyn, New York, Dr. Fauci has dedicated his career to the study and treatment of infectious diseases.

Dr Athul Gawande

Dr. Atul Gawande is an American surgeon, writer, and public health researcher known for his contributions to healthcare and his insightful writings on medicine and healthcare systems. Born on November 5, 1965, in Brooklyn, New York, Dr. Gawande has made significant impacts in the fields of surgery, public health, and medical journalism.

Dr Deepak Chopra

Deepak Chopra is an Indian-American author, public speaker, and alternative medicine advocate known for his holistic approach to health and spirituality. Born on October 22, 1946, in New Delhi, India, Chopra is one of the most prominent figures in the New Age movement and integrative medicine.

Dr Paul Farmer

Dr. Paul Farmer was an American physician, anthropologist, and global health advocate known for his groundbreaking work in providing healthcare to underserved populations around the world. Born on October 26, 1959, in North Adams, Massachusetts, Dr. Farmer dedicated his life to addressing healthcare disparities and promoting social justice.

Dr Andrew Weil

Dr. Andrew Weil is an American physician, author, and pioneer in integrative medicine, known for his holistic approach to health and wellness. Born on June 8, 1942, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Dr. Weil has become a leading advocate for combining conventional and alternative medicine practices to promote overall well-being.

Dr Michio Kaku

Dr. Michio Kaku is a renowned American theoretical physicist, futurist, and popular science communicator known for his work in theoretical physics and his ability to explain complex scientific concepts in accessible ways. Born on January 24, 1947, in San Jose, California, Dr. Kaku has made significant contributions to our understanding of the universe, particularly in the fields of string theory, quantum mechanics, and the physics of the future.
