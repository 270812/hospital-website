ABOUT MEDCARE

Medcare is a renowned medical institution in Italy, widely acknowledged for its excellence in medical education, healthcare services, and cutting-edge research. Established in 1956 in Italy, Medcare operates autonomously under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Italy. With a primary focus on producing highly skilled healthcare professionals, Medcare offers comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate medical education programs, including courses in nursing, dental surgery, and allied health sciences. The institution has consistently played a pivotal role in advancing medical research, contributing significantly to the fields of medicine and biomedical sciences.
